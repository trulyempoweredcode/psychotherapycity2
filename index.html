What I Can Help You With

You may already know something needs to shift.

In a warm, practical and collaborative way, I can help you to:

  • Untangle situations that feel complicated or overwhelming
  • Step back from the detail and see the bigger picture
  • Reflect on patterns, decisions and relationships
  • Explore new ways of responding to challenges
  • Strengthen your inner resources and resilience
  • Set and maintain healthy boundaries
  • Navigate or leave difficult relationships
  • Manage anxiety, stress and periods of uncertainty
  • Reconcile with past experiences
  • Move forward with greater confidence and purpose

Why Experience Matters

When you are facing significant decisions or emotional challenges, experience matters.

Over two decades of practice, alongside my earlier career in business, I have worked with people navigating:

Professional Life

Demanding professional careers. Entrepreneurial pressures and leadership responsibilities.

Personal Challenges

Complex family dynamics. Divorce and relationship breakdown. Loss and grief.

Life Transitions

Wealth and financial transitions. Questions of identity, meaning and direction.

This breadth of experience allows us to move beyond the surface of a problem and understand what is really going on beneath it.
